ECOSOC: coordination, agenda, and responsibility

ECOSOC | Youth participation and structured work

This entry focuses on disciplined participation, coordination, and the responsibility of representing youth voices in structured spaces.

Why ECOSOC matters

ECOSOC work highlights how formal processes operate through documentation, time frames, and precise interventions. Participating effectively requires preparation and a clear understanding of what can be supported with evidence.

Outcome

The experience strengthened my commitment to producing clear summaries, tracking key points, and communicating responsibly. These habits are essential when the audience includes institutions, partners, and stakeholders who rely on accuracy.
